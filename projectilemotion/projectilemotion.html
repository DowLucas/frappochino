Projectile Motion Sim

This is my projectile simulation. Play around with gravity (1st slider), velocity (2nd slider), angle (3rd slider), and the starting altitude (4th slider), and hit go! The the metrics provided at the top of the page calculates the predicted maximum altitude and the predicted range (distance). You can also view what gravity, velocity, angle, you have se and see how it impacts the projectile in flight.

OBS: setting gravity higher than 1 will make the projectile fall much faster. It will also slight mess up the predicted range calculations.
